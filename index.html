Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the top questions we get from families.

  • What exactly do you do as a college counselor?
    We guide students through the process—balanced list, essay coaching, application strategy, timelines, and financial aid basics—so you move from stress to strategy.
  • When should we start—9th, 10th, 11th grade?
    We can begin as early as 9th. Many start in 10th–11th for list building, test plan, and essays. Seniors welcome for focused, deadline-driven support.
  • Do you offer virtual or in-person meetings?
    Both. Virtual nationwide and in-person in the Charlotte / Lake Norman area when schedules allow.
  • How do packages and pricing work?
    Choose hourly help, an Essentials bundle (essays + list), or comprehensive support. We’ll recommend the right scope after a free consult.
  • Do you write essays for students?
    No—we coach brainstorming, structure, voice, and editing so the essay is authentically the student’s work.
